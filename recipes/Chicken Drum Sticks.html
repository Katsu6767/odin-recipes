Chicken Drum Sticks

These easy baked chicken drumsticks are crispy on the outside, and moist on the inside--a flavorful, cheap meal to make any time!

Ingredients

  • 6 (4 ounce) bone-in chicken drumsticks, with skin

  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter

  • Â½ cup extra-virgin olive oil, divided

  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste

  • 1 pinch seasoned salt, or to taste

  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  • Step 1:Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a glass baking dish with aluminum foil.

  • Step 2:Spread 1/4 cup of olive oil over the bottom of the prepared baking dish and line up drumsticks on top, alternating directions so that they fit. Top with 1 tablespoon of butter each, on the thick part of the chicken. Sprinkle with garlic salt, seasoned salt, salt, and pepper.

  • Step 3:Bake in the preheated oven for 35 minutes. Cover chicken with remaining olive oil; continue to bake until juices run clear and chicken is no longer pink in the centers, 10 to 15 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Serve.
